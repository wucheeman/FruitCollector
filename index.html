Fruit Collector!

Game Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game as a target number. Your goal is to generate a total score that matches it.

To win, your total score must match your target number exactly. You lose if your total score goes above the target.

There are four pieces of fruit below. When you click on one, you add some points to your total score for the game. The point value of each fruit is hidden until you click on it and look to see how much it increased your total score. This value does not change over the course of one round of the game. It does change each time a new round starts.

Good luck and happy counting!
